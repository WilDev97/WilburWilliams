About Me

Greetings! I'm Wilbur Williams, hailing from the picturesque St. Thomas in the US Virgin Islands. Throughout my life, I've been a dedicated athlete, proudly representing my home in international tennis tournaments across the Caribbean. This athletic journey instilled in me the invaluable lesson that hard work may not always result in immediate victory, but it undeniably paves the way for long-term success. In pursuit of my passion for technology, I embarked on a journey in computer science at Hunter College, aspiring to become a software engineer. However, as life often unfolds unexpected twists, I discovered that my true calling lies in cloud engineering. Taking a step back to reassess my path, I am now fervently working towards a rewarding career in cloud engineering. Currently on the lookout for entry-level opportunities or internships in the dynamic realms of cloud and data, I am eager to contribute my skills and passion to innovative projects. Beyond the professional realm, I find joy in delving into the world of Generative AI during my free time. I've been cultivating my skills and have exciting project outlines that intertwine the realms of data, cloud concepts, and the fascinating possibilities of Generative AI. As I navigate this journey, I am excited about the prospect of merging my athletic resilience, technological prowess, and newfound passion for Generative AI into a fulfilling and impactful career.
